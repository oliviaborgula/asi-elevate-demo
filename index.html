How to Earn

Explore additional activities and promotions to boost your ASI Elevate points and redeem exclusive rewards.

Distributors often struggle to stay consistently engaged with ASI's tools, training, and community. Many feel overwhelmed by the volume of resources or are unclear on how to maximize their membership value.

This point-based system is designed to encourage distributors to engage with ASI's offerings:

  • Attending webinars: 50 points per webinar.
  • Taking courses in ASI Learning System: 80 points per course
  • Placing orders through ESP+: 100 points per order
  • On-time payments: 150 points per on-time payment
  • Referring new members: 250 points per referral

Additional rewards include:

  • 10% discount on membership
  • 50 free catalogs
  • Free sample promo product
  • One free custom logo design
